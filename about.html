About Me

Get to know me!

I am currently a junior in college trying to pursue a major in Chinese and Media. I love travelling to all sorts of new places and experiencing new things. I am also an avid lover of music, different types of cuisines, animals. Follow me on my blog to gain tips and insights on all the places I go!

I acquired my love of travelling from my mother. Ever since I was a kid, my mother would take my sisters and I on different road trips. Most of our trips invovled visiting family members, but we would also go for vacation as wel.

Although travelling might seem scary and expensive for some people, I wanted to create this blog in order to help people overcome those fears. I hope my blog provides the courage and inspiration they need to get up and travel to somewhere new
